🐚 About ShellSearch

Our Mission

ShellSearch was founded in 1995 with a simple mission: to organize the World Wide Web and make it universally accessible. We index over 10,000 web pages and growing!

How It Works

Our patented Shell-Crawl™ technology scans the internet 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, cataloging every page it finds. Our advanced algorithms rank pages by relevance using proprietary Conch-Rank™ technology.

The Team

ShellSearch is a product of Conch Computing Inc., makers of ShellOS. Our team of 3 engineers works tirelessly from our garage office in Palo Alto to bring you the best search experience on the web.
